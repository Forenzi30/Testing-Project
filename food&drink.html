HOTEL 999 DINING

Our dining area is not just a convenient spot for guests, but a vibrant culinary destination located right below the hotel. Surrounded by the lively atmosphere of the local streets, this area offers a variety of delicious Indonesian dishes such as fried rice, capcay, bihun, kwetiau, and fresh seafood. Whether you’re looking for a late-night bite or a casual dinner, the warm ambiance and authentic flavors make it the perfect place to relax and enjoy your meal after a long day.

Decks & Common Areas

Lobby Area The hotel lobby is the first place that welcomes guests with a simple yet comfortable atmosphere. Here, guests can take a short break before continuing their activities or checking in.

Dining Area Located directly below the hotel, this area is a culinary center with a wide selection of local foods such as fried rice, capcay, vermicelli, kwetiau, and various seafood dishes. The atmosphere is lively and suitable for enjoying meals with family or friends, especially in the evening.

Street Corner Area Around the hotel, there are small streets where locals pass by. This is perfect for guests who want to experience everyday life and explore various stalls and shops around the hotel.

Parking & Gathering Spot There is a parking area and a simple gathering spot in front of the hotel, making it easy for guests who come by two-wheeled or four-wheeled vehicles.
